# Adava University - Hero Video Production Brief

## Overview
Create a premium, cinematic 45-50 second video for the homepage hero section that communicates the paradigm shift from traditional coding education to AI-first development.

---

## Video Specifications

**Duration:** 45-50 seconds  
**Format:** MP4, 1920x1080 (16:9)  
**Frame Rate:** 30fps  
**Style:** Cinematic, premium, aspirational  
**Tone:** Professional, formal, prestigious  
**Color Palette:** Adava brand colors (purple: #a259ff, teal: #25877D, dark: #0f0f1a)

---

## Production Tools

- **Veo 3:** Generate cinematic AI video shots
- **Banana Pro / ElevenLabs:** Professional voiceover generation
- **CapCut / DaVinci Resolve:** Video editing and compositing
- **Epidemic Sound / Artlist:** Background music (or AI-generated ambient tech music)

## Audio Specifications

### Voiceover Details
- **Gender:** Male (authoritative, trustworthy) OR Female (modern, aspirational) - your choice
- **Accent:** Neutral American or British English
- **Tone:** Professional, confident, aspirational but not salesy
- **Pace:** Moderate (not rushed, allows visuals to breathe)
- **Volume:** Clear and prominent but not overpowering music

**ElevenLabs/Banana Pro Settings:**
- Voice: "Professional Narrator" or "Corporate Explainer"
- Stability: 70-80% (natural but consistent)
- Clarity: 80-90%
- Style: 0-20% (neutral, professional)

### Background Music
- **Style:** Subtle, modern tech ambience
- **Characteristics:**
  - Soft electronic pads
  - Minimal melodic elements
  - Rising emotional arc (builds to CTA)
  - NOT distracting or overpowering
- **Volume:** 20-30% of voiceover level
- **Examples:** "Tech Innovation" or "Corporate Success" tracks on Epidemic Sound

---

## Color Grading

Apply consistent color grading across all scenes:

- **Shadows:** Lift slightly, add purple tint (#0f0f1a)
- **Midtones:** Neutral with slight teal push
- **Highlights:** Bright but not blown out
- **Saturation:** Moderate (not oversaturated)
- **Contrast:** Medium-high for cinematic feel

**Scene-Specific:**
- Scene 1: Desaturated, cooler tones
- Scenes 2-5: Vibrant, warmer with purple/teal accents

---

## Typography & Text Overlays

**Font:** Inter or SF Pro (clean, modern sans-serif)

**Text Overlays:**
- "10 Days" (Scene 3)
- "$94,000 Average Salary" (Scene 4)
- "3 Spots Remaining" (Scene 5)
- "Reserve Your Spot" (Final CTA)

**Animation Style:**
- Fade in with slight scale (1.05x to 1.0x)
- Duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds
- No bouncing or overly playful animations (maintain formal tone)

---

## Transitions

**Between Scenes:**
- Smooth cross-dissolve (0.5-0.75 seconds)
- OR elegant wipe with brand color accent
- Avoid: Hard cuts, jarring transitions
